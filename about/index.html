STUY 3D is a registered club of Stuyvesant High School, in Manhattan, New York City. We provide a flexible and enabling environment for those who want to pursue projects involving 3D-printing. With access to professional equipment and expertise, we’re dedicated to spreading knowledge and usage of 3D-printing.
